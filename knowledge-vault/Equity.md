# Equity

**What it is:** Direct ownership of company shares traded on stock exchanges (NSE / BSE).

## Key Facts
- Requires DEMAT + Trading account (via a registered broker)
- Regulated by [[SEBI Regulations]]
- Historical long-term CAGR ~12–15% (Sensex)
- High short-term volatility — capital loss is possible

## Risk & Goals
- Risk: [[Risk High]]
- Suits: [[Long-term Goal]] (7+ years only)

## Best For
[[Long-term Goals]] · [[High Risk Appetite]] · [[Experienced Investors]]

> ⚠️ Not suitable for short time horizons or low-risk profiles.
