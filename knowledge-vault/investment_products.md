## [[Fixed Deposit]]

**What it is:** A savings instrument where you deposit a lump sum at a guaranteed rate for a fixed tenure.

**Key facts:**
- DICGC insurance up to ₹5 lakh per bank per depositor
- Tenure: 7 days – 10 years
- Premature withdrawal allowed (with penalty)
- Interest taxable as per income slab

**Best for:** [[Capital Preservation]], [[Short-term Goals]], [[Senior Citizens]]

**Related:** [[Recurring Deposit]] | [[Risk: Low]]

## [[Recurring Deposit]]

**What it is:** Monthly fixed-amount deposits accumulating with compound interest.

**Key facts:**
- Entry from ₹100/month
- Builds savings discipline
- Returns guaranteed (bank sets rate at opening)
- Tenure: 6 months – 10 years

**Best for:** [[Salaried Professionals]], [[First-time Savers]], [[Emergency Fund Building]]

**Related:** [[Fixed Deposit]] | [[Risk: Low]]

## [[Bonds]]

**What it is:** Debt instruments where investors lend to government or corporations.

**Types:**
- **G-Secs (Government Securities):** Sovereign guarantee, lowest risk
- **PSU Bonds:** Public sector undertakings, near-sovereign safety
- **Corporate Bonds:** Higher yield, credit risk varies

**Best for:** [[Steady Income]], [[Medium-term Goals]], [[Risk: Low to Medium]]

**Related:** [[Mutual Funds]] | [[Fixed Income]]

## [[Mutual Funds]]

**What it is:** Pooled investment vehicle managed by SEBI-registered AMCs.

**Types:**
| Type | Risk | Best for |
|------|------|----------|
| Equity Funds | High | Long-term wealth creation |
| Debt Funds | Low-Medium | Short/medium-term, capital preservation |
| Hybrid Funds | Medium | Balanced portfolio |
| Index Funds | Medium | Low-cost market tracking |

**SIP:** Systematic Investment Plan — invest from ₹500/month.

**Disclaimer:** Mutual fund investments are subject to market risks.

**Best for:** [[Long-term Goals]], [[Diversification]], [[Medium to High Risk]]

**Related:** [[Equity]] | [[SEBI Regulations]]

## [[Equity]]

**What it is:** Direct ownership of company shares via stock exchanges (NSE/BSE).

**Key facts:**
- Requires DEMAT + Trading account
- Regulated by SEBI
- Highest historical long-term returns (~12-15% CAGR Sensex)
- High short-term volatility

**Risk:** Capital loss possible. Not suitable for short time horizons.

**Best for:** [[Long-term Goals]] (7+ years), [[High Risk Appetite]], [[Experienced Investors]]

**Related:** [[Mutual Funds]] | [[Risk: High]] | [[Capital Appreciation]]
