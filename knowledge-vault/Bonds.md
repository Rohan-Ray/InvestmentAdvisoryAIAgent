# Bonds

**What it is:** Debt instruments where investors lend to government or corporations in exchange for regular interest (coupon) payments.

## Types
| Type | Risk | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| G-Secs (Government Securities) | Lowest | Sovereign guarantee |
| PSU Bonds | Low | Public sector undertakings |
| Corporate Bonds | Medium | Higher yield, credit risk varies |
