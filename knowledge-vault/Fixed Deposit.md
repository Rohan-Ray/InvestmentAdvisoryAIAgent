# Fixed Deposit

**What it is:** A savings instrument where you deposit a lump sum at a guaranteed rate for a fixed tenure.

## Key Facts
- DICGC insurance up to ₹5 lakh per bank per depositor
- Tenure: 7 days – 10 years
- Premature withdrawal allowed (with penalty)
- Interest taxable as per income slab
