# SEBI Regulations

SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India) is the primary capital-markets regulator.

## Key Regulations
- **Investment Advisors:** Must be SEBI-registered (IA Regulations 2013)
- **AMCs:** Regulated under SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations 1996
- **Exchanges:** NSE and BSE — regulated and supervised by SEBI
- **Equity Trading:** All brokers and DEMAT custodians registered with SEBI
