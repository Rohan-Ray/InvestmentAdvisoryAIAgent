# Recurring Deposit

**What it is:** Monthly fixed-amount deposits accumulating with compound interest.

## Key Facts
- Entry from ₹100/month
- Builds savings discipline
- Returns guaranteed (bank sets rate at opening)
- Tenure: 6 months – 10 years
