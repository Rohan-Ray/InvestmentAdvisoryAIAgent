# Mutual Funds

**What it is:** Pooled investment vehicle managed by SEBI-registered Asset Management Companies (AMCs).

## Types
| Type | Risk | Best For |
|------|------|----------|
| Equity Funds | High | Long-term wealth creation |
| Debt Funds | Low-Medium | Short/medium-term, capital preservation |
| Hybrid Funds | Medium | Balanced portfolio |
| Index Funds | Medium | Low-cost market tracking |

## SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)
Invest from ₹500/month automatically — ideal for [[Salaried Professionals]].

All AMCs are registered with [[SEBI Regulations]] (MF Regulations 1996).
